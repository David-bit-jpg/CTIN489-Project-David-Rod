Enrich your horror game with this ghost pack,with more than 12 variation of ghosts and 12 Basic animations.Now, included some funny ghost variations to make your game more attractive.
This pack contains 3d low poly Animated model of a Ghost.Pack comes with many color variation and animations.You can use this pack commercially and non-commercially.

Technical Details:-
		- Model(s) : Ghost, Axe
		- Vertex count : 1157(Ghost), 600(Axe)
		- Tris count :  2254(Ghost), 1196(Axe)
		- Unwrapped : Yes
		- Texture Variations : 12 (8 Scary ghosts and 4 Funny ghost)
		- Animations : 12 (Idle, look, Attack_1, Attack_2, hit, death, crouch, fly/run, follow_path, dance_1, dance_2, weird)
		- Used Material(s) : Unity Standard Material
		- Texture maps : Albedo/Base Color, Normal, Metalic Smoothness
		- Texture Size : 2048(.png)
		

Features:-
	- Lots of unique texture variations
	- Scay, funny & cute ghosts
	- Many high quality animations
	- Colliders, Rigidbodies attached
	- PBR Textures
	- Mobile, AR supported

	Materials : Where the materials for the model located.

	Textures : Contains Textures, that are in (.PNG) file format
	
	(Other folders - Prefabs, Scenes, Animations)
